Ingredients:
 - 1 stick of butter
 - 2 lbs of flour
 - 3 tablespoons of sugar
 - 1/2 cup milk
 - 2/3 cup of water

Directions:
  1. Add all ingredients into bowl and mix thoroughly
  2. Knead the dough and place into round balls on a cooking sheet
  3. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit
  4. Place dough in the oven for 25 minutes
  5. Check to see if dough is a light golden brown and serve hot
